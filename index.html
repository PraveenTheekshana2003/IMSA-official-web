Who We Are ?

The Industrial Mathematics Students’ Association (IMSA) is a society dedicated to enhance the knowledge and practical applications of mathematics among its members. Collaborating with industry, IMSA focuses on promoting industrial mathematics and its relevance in real-world problem-solving. This association provides a platform for mathematics undergraduates of the Faculty to connect, exchange ideas, and learn from one another. By actively engaging undergraduates, IMSA contributes in bridging the gap between academia and industry, equipping its members for successful careers in industrial mathematics and related fields. Additionally, IMSA is committed to disseminate mathematics knowledge to the general public by organizing various activities to support mathematics learners, including school children.

IMSA is a student community where math meets real-world applications, helping students learn, grow, and connect beyond the classroom.
